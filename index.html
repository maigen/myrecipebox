Seared Ahi Tuna with Sesame Seed Crust

Mix the Sesame Crust ingredients together in a small bowl. Pat dry the ahi tuna with paper towels.
Place ahi tuna on a plate, coat all sides with soy sauce. This will help the sesame spice adhere to the tuna.





Stuffed Dates with Pistachios

Using your fingers, gently split dates in two halves and remove pit. Place on a serving platter, opening them up to better hold the filling.
Using a pastry bag or spoon, fill dates with creme fraiche. Push a few pistachios or nut of choice into the filling.

Peruvian Ceviche

Place the sliced onion in a bowl of generously salted water to soak (to remove bitterness) Finely chop the garlic, chili pepper and ginger.
Add the salt and using the flat surface of a chef’s knife mash it together until it becomes a fine paste.
